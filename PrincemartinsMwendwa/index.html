Enterpreneurship

Entrepreneurship refers to an individual or a small group of partners who strike out on an original path to create a new business.

Importance of Enterpreneurship

Enterpreneurship is important for the following reasons;

  1. Economic Growth:

    Entrepreneurs play a crucial role in driving economic growth and innovation by creating job opportunities and contribute to the GDP and tax revenues of a country

  2. Wealth Generation:

    Entrepreneurs frequently target new markets and tap audiences outside the focus of established firms which creates new sources of revenue and profits which benefits both themselves and the broader economy.

  3. Social Change:

    The innovative goods and services offered by entrepreneurs reduce dependence on outdated processes and technologies.For example, smartphones have revolutionized how businesses communicate with customers, employees, and partners which drives positive social change by challenging the status quo and introducing better solutions
